Stock Popularity & Cross Attention
During this part, we compute how many inverstors followers a give target stock(left), Also, we can further compute how many inverstors followers another stock given that they have follower a given target stock(right). From Behavior Finance, we know that if two stock are followed synergistically, these two stock must follow some pattern in their stock market. The default stock code is 000010. stock 000010 is a compnay of achitechive &decoration industry, in Chinese "建筑装饰". So the left figure is the stock popularity in achitechive &decoration industry. you can get a sense of how popular stock 000010 is in its industry. the right figure show that number of the intersection of the follower set of another stock and the stock 000010 follower set. you can find that stock 300104( a media company) and 000010 has the largest numbe, 10344 users in common. So 300104, 000010 has some relationship in stock market from Behavior Finance theory. you can also type 000002,600010 etc.
